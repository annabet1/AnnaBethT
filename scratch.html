    Steps to the Floral Process

    1. Quality Flowers
      • Select fresh, high-quality flowers from a reputable supplier.
      • Choose a variety of flowers to create a visually appealing arrangement.
      • Ensure flowers are properly hydrated and stored to maintain their freshness.
    2. Quality Arrangement
      • Use clean, sharp tools to trim stems and remove excess leaves.
      • Arrange flowers in a balanced and aesthetically pleasing manner.
      • Incorporate greenery and filler flowers to enhance the overall look.
    3. Quality Presentation
      • Select a suitable vase or container that complements the arrangement.
      • Add decorative elements like ribbons or stones to elevate the presentation.
      • Display the arrangement in a cool, well-lit area to prolong its beauty.
